## Course Information

**Course Code:** CHEM 301  
**Level:** Graduate  
**Semester:** Summer 2026
**Credits:** 1

## Description

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to classical thermodynamics and its applications in chemistry and materials science. Students will learn fundamental concepts including the laws of thermodynamics, thermodynamic potentials, phase equilibria, and chemical equilibrium.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand the four laws of thermodynamics and their applications
- Apply thermodynamic principles to real-world problems
- Analyze phase diagrams and predict phase behavior
- Calculate thermodynamic properties of chemical systems
- Evaluate chemical equilibria using thermodynamic data
